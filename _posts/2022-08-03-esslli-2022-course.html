We (James Pustejovsky and Nikhil Krishnaswamy) are pleased to be teaching a week-long summer course from August 8-12, 2022, at the European Summer School in Logic, Language, and Information in Galway, Ireland! Our course is entitled Multimodal Semantics for Affordances and Actions, and is based on our Best Paper-winning paper from HCII 2022, of the same title. The course is part of the Language and Computation track at ESSLLI. The slides, Zoom recordings, and relevant background readings for the five lectures are below:
