As part of the 16th Joint ACL - ISO Workshop on Interoperable Semantic Annotation (ISA), taking place at LREC 2020 in Marseille, France, we are soliciting image annotations as part of a proposal to develop VoxML into an ISO Standard for the representation of visual information. More information about ISA and the VoxML special track can be found here and the dataset for the shared task is posted here.
